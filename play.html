How to Play

Join EarthWorldMC on your preferred platform

  • Java
  • Bedrock
  • Bedrock (Console)

To join on Minecraft Java Edition enter the IP play.earthworldmc.net with the port 25565

To join on Minecraft Bedrock Edition enter the IP play.earthworldmc.net

To join on Minecraft Bedrock (PS/Xbox) Edition download BedrockTogether. It should look like this

Enter play.earthworldmc.net in to the IP box and press start/run. Your mobile device must be on the same WiFi network as your gaming console for this to work. Open Minecraft on your PlayStation or Xbox and navigate to the friends tab. At the bottom there should be an entry for BedrockTogether. Click on it to join and it will load into the server. Once you are in EarthWorldMC you can press the stop button on the app on your mobile device or close it.
